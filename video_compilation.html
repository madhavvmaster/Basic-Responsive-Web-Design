Why Indian Rupee is Falling?

The Indian rupee is falling mainly due to trade deal uncertainity with the US, foreign capital outflows and global tariff pressures. Analysts highlight that delays in the India - US trade agreement, aggressive sellg by foreign investors and presistent demand for the US dollar have made the rupee one of Asia's worst-perfroming currencies in 2025.
